Begin to study Bachelor of Computer Science in Vietnamese-German University in September 2017. Came from Ly Tu Trong Gifted High School of Can Tho, majoring in Informatics.

Previously, in high school, I was mostly solving problems in Algorithmic Contests. I excitingly moved to programming softwares and websites, both for open-source companies and private start-ups. My favorite programming languages include Python, C++, JavaScript.

Since 2017, I have been actively joining in multiple projects and activities, including Google Code-in, PiMA, AlgoTrading, etc. as a long-term contributor. Besides, I'm writing a series of blogs and algorithmic tutorials for newcomers which may inspire them to learn Code and use it to build the future.

Second prize in the 2017 Vietnam Olympiad of Informatics, ranking #34.
Bronze medal in the 2014 National ViOlmypic.

I love Computer, Arts, Mathematics, Meme, Music, Animals, but not necessarily in that order.

Born and grew up in Can Tho, Vietnam, moved to Binh Duong, Vietnam for university.
